Why PETIT PEMBROKES daycare nursery?

We are a 60 place Day Care Nursery open 52 weeks per year. Our opening times are 8am to 6pm Monday to Friday.

The Nursery is ideally located for commuters as we are within walking distance to Bromley South, Bromley North and Bickley train stations.

Our primary goal is to ensure that we provide the highest quality childcare that is both accessible and affordable.

Our experienced and qualified staff provide a safe, secure and stimulating environment for the children. We also benefit from a large indoor playroom, music area and secure outdoor garden for the children to access throughout the day. The children are also encouraged to learn basic sign language.
