PETIT PEMBROKES SUMMER CLUB!

Petit Pembrokes is pleased to announce the introduction of our Summer Club for 2020.
As we continue to provide childcare throughout this pandemic we recognise a demand for care through the summer months as families continue to juggle the challenges of work, home life and childcare.
With the government’s step 3 of the recovery strategy coming into effect from 4 July we are able to provide Summer Club activities for primary aged children ahead of their return to school in September.
Activities will satisfy the Covid Secure guidelines as we are able to provide dedicated indoor play rooms and toilet provision completely separate from our pre school nursery setting along with outdoor play space. Dedicated staff will provide the care.
We are offering Summer Club from Monday 6th July throughout the summer with proposed hours of 9am to 3pm.
Prices per day are £35 and each child is to bring a packed lunch (meeting government guidance).
If you are interested in reserving a place at our summer club then please contact our Manager Rachel Harris; if you are interested in hours outside of the 9am-3pm proposed hours then we are happy to discuss this with you.
